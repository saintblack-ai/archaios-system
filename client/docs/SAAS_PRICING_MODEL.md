# SaaS Pricing Model (Prep)

## Recommended Pricing Bands
- Free: `$0/month`
- Pro: `$49/month` (launch default, within target band `$29–$49`)
- Elite: `$99/month` (launch default, within target band `$79–$99`)

## Pricing Logic
- Keep one canonical launch price per paid tier for clarity:
  - Pro launch: `$49`
  - Elite launch: `$99`
- Use lower-band values (`$29`, `$79`) only for controlled promo tests or limited-time offers.

## Packaging Rationale
- Free captures intent and list growth without billing friction.
- Pro is the main conversion tier for recurring revenue.
- Elite is the high-value tier for urgency-driven operators and deeper analytics access.

## Local Prep Constraints
- Stripe remains test/prep mode.
- No billing activation.
- No production secret changes.
